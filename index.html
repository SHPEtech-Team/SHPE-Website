Empowering Latinx in Engineering

Welcome to SHPE at UCR! We are a student chapter for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers. We aim to empower Latinx individuals pursuing engineering careers through a diverse range of opportunities. Whether you're a student seeking guidance, a professional looking to give back, or simply someone interested in our cause, you've come to the right place. Explore our offerings and become part of the SHPE at UCR familia. Together, we'll make a meaningful impact in the world of engineering.

SHPEtinas Conference

We are extremely excited to announce our upcoming SHPEtinas Conference! This year's conference will focus on inspiring Community College, and University students who are interested in pursuing different career paths in tech. All genders are welcome and encouraged to attend and participate. We will be providing workshops and speaker panels hosted by various tech companies and organizations! Please make sure to sign up through our Eventbrite!
